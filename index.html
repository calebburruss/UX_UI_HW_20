Timeline

Three Weeks

We observed that Habitat for Humanity's website for their local office in Las Vegas, Nevada does not offer a design that engages users to utilize HFH as a resource to find housing.
Through User Research we were able to discover what users were looking for when Using the Habitat for Humanity Website and we were able to identify the problems that users had while using the site.
70% of Users had difficulties when looking for application information for housing on the Habitat for Humanity Website.
My team created User Surveys and conducted User Interviews to get a better inderstanding of how people felt and understood the information they were seeing.

Habitat for Humanity in Las Vegas offers helpful and accomodating housing for families in need. Unforunately the housing application information is not easily accessible
THE SOLUTION
We decided as a team to focus on the overall design of the HFH LV website and to highlight the housing assistance of the nonprofit. By streamlining the process to find housing information and organize said information in a more user-friendly format it will be that much easier for users to get the help they need.
